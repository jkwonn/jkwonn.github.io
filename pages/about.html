About Us

Hello, my name is Justin Kwon, and I'm currently a freshman in Purdue University's First Year Engineering program, looking to major in Computer Engineering.

My current pursuits are my YouTube channel where I work to inspire other students through showing off technology and processes that I enjoy, as well as my work as an Undergraduate Research Assistant at the Kim Lab. Our current research project focuses on researching inequalities in service provided to rural vs. urban areas, which I am contributing to through data scraping and processing.

My interests range from cameras, to ThinkPads, to keyboards, but I'm sure all of them will show up somewhere eventually. Thanks for taking a peek at my stuff!
